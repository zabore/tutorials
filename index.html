Survival analysis in R: A detailed tutorial on conducting survival analyses in R, including an introduction to the basics of survival analysis, landmark analyses and time-dependent covariates, competing risks, and a selection of advanced topics.

Creating websites in R: A basic introduction to creating simple websites in RMarkdown, using a website for my cats as an example. Note that this tutorial is most useful for a basic guide to hosting a website on Github, and is out of date on the newest available software like distill, Quarto, and others.
